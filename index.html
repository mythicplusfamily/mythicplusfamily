About Us

We are a guild that welcomes players of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned veterans. Our focus is on pushing keys as high as possible and achieving Keystone Master and Keystone Hero titles for players who want to participate.

Weekly Guild Events

Join us every Monday at 7pm EST for our weekly guild event. These events are a fantastic opportunity to meet fellow guild members, participate in exciting activities, and push those keys! Whether you're a seasoned key-pusher or just starting your journey in Azeroth, there's a place for you here.

Pushing Keys

Our guild is dedicated to pushing keys as high as possible. We believe in challenging ourselves and constantly improving our skills. Whether you're looking to push your first key or aiming for +10 or higher, our guild is here to support you.

Keystone Master and Keystone Hero

For those who are interested, we have groups running most evenings with the goal of achieving the Keystone Master and Keystone Hero titles. These titles are a testament to a player's skill and dedication, and we're proud to have numerous title holders in our guild.

Join Us

Whether you're a casual player or want to see how high you can push, there's a place for you in our guild. We believe in fostering a friendly and supportive environment where everyone can enjoy World of Warcraft at their own pace. So why wait? Join us and start your adventure today!
